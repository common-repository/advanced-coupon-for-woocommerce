== Description ==

The "Advanced Coupon for WooCommerce" plugin is your ultimate solution for implementing tiered discount structures seamlessly within your WooCommerce store. This powerful tool empowers store managers to create dynamic discount tiers based on various conditions, including cart subtotal, cart item quantity, cart weight, weekly days, date and time range, city, zipcode, or country, along with role-based discounts.

With the "Advanced Coupon for WooCommerce" plugin, you have unparalleled flexibility in setting up discount tiers tailored precisely to your business needs. Whether you want to incentivize bulk purchases, reward loyal customers, or run targeted promotions, this plugin offers the versatility and control you require.

== Key Features ==

**Flexible Tiered Pricing:** Configure tiered discount structures based on multiple conditions to suit your unique business requirements.
**Customizable Conditions:** Set up discounts based on cart subtotal, cart item quantity, cart weight, weekly days, date and time range, city, zipcode, country, or user roles.
**Dynamic Discounts:** Automatically apply tiered discounts based on the specified conditions, ensuring a seamless and personalized shopping experience for your customers.
**Easy Configuration:** Access and manage tiered discount settings directly from the WooCommerce coupon page, simplifying the setup process for store managers.
**Enhanced Customer Engagement:** Attract and retain customers by offering targeted discounts that align with their purchasing behavior and preferences.
**Boost Sales:** Drive revenue growth by leveraging tiered pricing strategies to stimulate customer spending and increase average order value.


== Usage ==

Set up your desired discount tiers and specify the conditions under which each tier should apply.
Save your changes to activate the tiered discount structure for your WooCommerce store.
Customers will automatically receive discounts based on the configured conditions when they meet the specified criteria during checkout.
Monitor the performance of your tiered discounts and adjust settings as needed to optimize your sales strategy and maximize profitability.
